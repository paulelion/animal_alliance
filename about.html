Our Rescuers

These individuals run the Animal Alliance kennel operation. The dogs entrusted to their care receive specialized attention from this highly skilled group of dog lovers who have chosen a profession in animal sheltering. The high standards of sanitation and enrichment in the Animal Alliance shelter are the cornerstones of all work done on behalf of the dogs awaiting adoption.
